General API information

VoyageOne OpenAPI is a program that enables channel partners to integrate with Voyageone’s e-commerce platform, and ultimately our sellers. The goal of this program is to connect online sellers with new sources of demand.

Once in the VoyageOne OpenAPI program, channel partners will build an HTTP API to the specifications outlined in this portal and expose common e-commerce operations in a standardized form to Voyageone. Voyageone will then utilize this API to synchronize product and order data on behalf of retailers.

The integration model is intended to work for a variety of channels and processes. At a high level, we will be sending requests and receiving information from the channel on:

  • Adding or updating product content
  • Updates to quantity and price
  • Enabling or disabling products on the site
  • Orders
  • Shipment details
  • Cancellations/Refunds

Additional details and technical specifications can be found in the API Documentation pages. If after review of the specifications you have additional questions specific to your channel, or have a process that does not fit into the predefined workflow, reach out to your business development contact for more information.
