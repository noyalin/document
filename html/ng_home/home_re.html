Requirements

Requirements page outlines in detail what deliverables are required for each marketplace.

Along with the technical integration and requirements, there is basic information that Voyageone will need to be able to create a template for sellers to provide product data in your required format. We also require additional information from you that will enable us to communicate the value of your site to our sellers and to prepare them to connect to your program through Voyageone.

You should start preparing the following deliverables as soon as your agreement is signed with Voyageone. It is also important to note that we cannot commence Voyageone development as outlined in the process flow until we receive the following information; any delays in receiving these details will push out your potential go-live date.

Partner Deliverable Checklist:

  • Channel Questionnaire for Marketing and Business Development
  • Channel Questionnaire for Configuration
  • Category,Taxonomy & Template Documentation
    • Engineering team require XSDs or similar for taxonomy
    • Business Development & Account Management require an excel version of this to share with sellers
    • Both version should be submitted to our Partner Support team
      (partnersupport@Voyageone.com)
  • Template Definition (details: Home>Requirements>Template Definition)
  • Test Account Credentials: We require 4 different sets of credentials. These will be used in several testing environments as well as a set for production.
